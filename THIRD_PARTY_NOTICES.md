# Third-party avatar assets

`entry/src/main/resources/base/media/avatar_male.png` and
`entry/src/main/resources/base/media/avatar_female.png` are OpenMoji artwork
from https://github.com/hfg-gmuend/openmoji, licensed under CC BY-SA 4.0.

The project’s existing health icons are reused for the default health-object
avatar choices.
